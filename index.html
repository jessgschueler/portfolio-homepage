Background

I come to programming from a non-traditional background. I have a Bachelor of Arts from Western Washington University in political science. After college I worked in e-commerce for a few years before I took some time off to travel. When I returned from travelling in early 2020, like many of us, I stayed inside.

Covid-19 lockdown really made me reevaluate my career path so far. Between attempts to bake sourdough and zoom game nights, I started to teach myself python. When I moved to Portland, I decided to get more serious about my love of programming and that led me to the Epicodus March 2022 cohort.
